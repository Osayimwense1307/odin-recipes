The recipe for Egusi soup

This is a brief recipe on making a nice, well and delicious melon soup. A well guided path on how to prepare it also.

INGREDIENTS

  • Melon
  • leaf
  • fish
  • meat
  • palm oil
  • tomatoes
  • water
  • love
  • pot
  • stock

STEPS

  1. Firstly,boil your meat with the stuck
  2. heat your red oil a lil bit and pour your meat stock
  3. allow to boil then stir and add your melon
  4. then wash and apply your leaf
  5. stir well and serve hot
